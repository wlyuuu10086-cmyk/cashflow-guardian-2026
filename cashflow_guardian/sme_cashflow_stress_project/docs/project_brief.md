# Project Brief: SME Cash Flow Stress Early Warning System

## Positioning
Commercial banking / fintech / SME lending / post-loan risk monitoring.

## Objective
Build an SME cash flow stress early warning system that helps financial institutions identify companies with cash flow pressure earlier and design differentiated intervention strategies.

## Core Business Questions
1. Which businesses are currently showing signs of cash flow stress?
2. What are the early warning signals of cash flow deterioration?
3. How do revenue decline, expense increase, collection delay, and higher loan repayment burden affect risk?
4. Are there significant differences in cash flow volatility across industries, regions, and business sizes?
5. Can cash flow indicators predict future delinquency risk in advance?
6. How should financial institutions apply different intervention strategies to businesses with different risk levels?
